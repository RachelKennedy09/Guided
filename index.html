About Guided

We strive to offer a safe space in which together we can seek deeper connection with oneself and explore what you may be seeking, be that reduced suffering, growth, understanding, or healing.

Through an emotionally and attachment focused lens, along with utilizing acceptance and commitment and psychodynamic therapy tools, we can work with you through a traditional psychotherapy framework or with the assistance of psychedelic medicines.

We have years of personal and professional experience with psychedelics and are grateful these medicines are now becoming more accessible as one avenue to help with mental health conditions.

Dr. Christine Kennedy, MD, FRCPC Psychiatry

Thank you for your curiosity and seeking an avenue of healing that feels aligned with you.

I am grateful to live an work on the unceded traditional territory of the Lheidli T'enneh Nation. I was raised in the North and am honored to continue to serve and work with people in Northern BC. I am a psychiatrist and in my general practice I offer psychodynamic therapy with a relational approach, focussing on attachment and emotional processing. I also have special interest and training in supporting pregnant and postpartum individuals.

I am passionate about the use of psychedelics as a way of opening one's heart and mind to what lies beyond their defenses and suffering. Psychedelics can be a psycho spiritual experience and for many people psychedelics have helped them reach deeper parts of themselves. Then with further integration these experiences can lead to true healing and growth in their lives and personal relationships.

I look forward to seeing if working together in this realm is the right fit at the right time.

Dr. Laura Fisher, MD, FRCPC

Guided Services

Currently we offer Ketamine Assisted Therapy for a variety of mental health diagnoses. We also offer psilocybin or MDMA assisted therapy for those that qualify for these treatments through Canada's Special Access Program. We are happy to help you connect to someone that will help you apply.

As many of our offerings are led by a psychiatrist, MSP covers a portion. However there are still costs associated with these forms of treatments. Some insurance companies can provide some benefit so ensure you check with yours.
